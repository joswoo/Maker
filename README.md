## Abstract
HOLO is an app that solves the loneliness of a single person. The HOLO logo is a must-have app for anyone who lives alone, sleeping and living. The logo embodies people's hugging with H to express loneliness and healing.
 
## Research
We selected one male and one female from the list of student and a worker living alone. And conducted a preliminary interview. Interviews resulted in many problems such as food, security, courier service, and loneliness. I tried to solve the loneliness common to all of them by application.
